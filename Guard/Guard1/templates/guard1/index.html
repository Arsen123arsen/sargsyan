Welcome,

Citiguard – We Create Safe Communities. Citiguard Security is proud of its reputation and unsurpassed standards in customer service.

Our goal is to partner with our clients to create a win for the client and the community.

Our pro-active mindset and ability to be flexible has resulted in a blue chip client list that is the envy of security providers. Whether protecting our clients' brands, premises, assets or people, no one else delivers the Citiguard promise.

Our officers are committed to providing a safe and secure environment for your employees, clients, tenants & vendors. We take special care in ensuring that you receive the right officer to fit your individual needs.

We strive to always put “the right person in the right place at the right time”. Our officers receive training that far exceeds the State of California minimum requirements.

Our officers are trained to proactively deter, observe and detect potential problems before they negatively affect your business or property.

Our officers receive individualized post orders and on-site training tailored to your specific needs. Our officers are our greatest assets. Officers are supported by a 24-hour fully staffed and equipped dedicated in-house dispatch center located at our Woodland Hills headquarters. Management & supervision are always on hand to address incidents and emergencies via the most advanced communication systems available.
